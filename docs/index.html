Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Catharsis Agent ("we," "our," or "the Application") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our automated social media posting service.

2. Information We Collect

Our Application collects and processes the following information:

  • Social Media Account Access: We require access tokens to post content on your behalf to Twitter (X) and Instagram accounts
  • Content Data: Generated text and images created by our AI system for posting
  • Authentication Credentials: API keys and access tokens necessary for social media platform integration

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Posting automated content to your connected social media accounts (Twitter/X and Instagram)
  • Generating AI-powered text and images for posts
  • Maintaining scheduled posting functionality
  • Tracking posting statistics and metrics

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information:

  • Access tokens and credentials are stored securely as environment variables
  • Generated content and posting state is stored in a secure file system
  • We do not collect, store, or access any personal information beyond what is necessary for posting functionality
  • We do not share your data with any third parties except the social media platforms themselves (Meta/Instagram and Twitter/X)

5. Third-Party Services

Our Application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For generating post text and images using GPT-4 and DALL-E 3
  • Meta/Instagram: For posting content to Instagram
  • Twitter/X: For posting content to Twitter
  • ImgBB: For temporary image hosting

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We encourage you to review their policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain operational data (posting state, statistics) only as long as necessary for the Application to function. Access tokens remain valid according to the expiration periods set by the respective social media platforms (typically 60 days for Instagram, indefinite for Twitter until revoked).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your social media accounts at any time through the respective platform's settings
  • Request deletion of any stored data by discontinuing use of the Application
  • Access information about what data we store related to your accounts

8. Children's Privacy

Our Application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

10. Data Processing for Instagram Content Publishing

When using our Instagram posting functionality:

  • We process images and captions provided by our AI generation system
  • Images are temporarily hosted on ImgBB to comply with Instagram's API requirements
  • We use Instagram Graph API to create and publish media containers
  • Published content is subject to Instagram's Terms of Service and Data Policy

11. Automated Decision Making

Our Application uses automated systems to:

  • Generate content using AI models (OpenAI GPT-4 and DALL-E 3)
  • Schedule and publish posts at predetermined intervals
  • Select content topics from a predefined rotation

These automated decisions do not involve any personal data about you beyond what is necessary for posting functionality.

13. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your information is:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent by authorizing our Application to access your social media accounts
  • Legitimate Interest: Operating the automated posting service you have requested

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time by revoking our Application's access through your social media account settings.
